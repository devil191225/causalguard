# Disclaimer & Legal Notice

## Purpose
CausalGuard is an academic research implementation of causal anomaly detection
on industrial control system (ICS) telemetry, evaluated on the SWaT dataset.
This is a **defensive research tool** for understanding and improving ICS
security posture.

## Authorized Use
- ✅ Academic research and publications
- ✅ Deploying anomaly detection on ICS/SCADA systems you own or are authorized to monitor
- ✅ Studying causal inference methods in security contexts

- ❌ Using anomaly detection evasion insights to attack industrial systems
- ❌ Any use against critical infrastructure without explicit authorization

## ICS/SCADA Notice
Industrial control systems underpin critical infrastructure. Any unauthorized
testing or interference with ICS/SCADA systems may violate laws including
the CFAA, and can endanger human safety. **Never test against live OT environments
without proper authorization and change management procedures.**
